About Lorain County Community College

Lorain County Community College is a public institution in Elyria, Ohio. Its campus is located in a city with a total enrollment of 11,042. The school utilizes a semester-based academic year. The student-faculty ratio is 16-to-1. The highest degree offered at Lorain County Community College is an associate degree. The school has an open admissions policy and offers credit for life experiences.

The in-district tuition and fees for 2017-2018 were $3,151, in-state tuition and fees were $3,753, and out-of-state tuition and fees were $7,376. There is no application fee. Need more info on tuiton? Check out the LCCC tuition calculator!

Students can earn degrees and certificates in 24 different fields. Popular programs include: Liberal Arts and Sciences, General Studies and Humanities, Health Professions and Related Programs, and Business, Management, Marketing, and Related Support Services.
